QUALITY POLICY
Customer Satisfaction: We adopt a customer-focused approach and keep customer satisfaction at the highest level. We strive to understand customer needs, meet 
their expectations and add value to them.
Customer Communication and Feedback: We regularly communicate to strengthen customer relationships. We take customer feedbacks into consideration and 
continuously measure customer satisfaction. We take corrective and preventive actions to increase customer satisfaction.
Continuous Improvement: We continuously review and analyze our business processes and seek opportunities for improvement. To achieve our quality targets, we
continuously improve ourselves and adopt an innovative approach.
Compliance with Quality Standards: We ensure full compliance with the ISO 9001 quality management standard and continuously improve our Quality Management 
System. We provide confidence to customers by complying with the applicable legal regulations and relevant standards.
Productivity and Efficiency: We manage our business processes productively and efficiently. We use resources accurately, optimize processes and aim for operational 
excellence.
Employee Development and Participation: We provide our employees with training and development opportunities to improve their capabilities. We encourage the 
participation of employees, assess their ideas and involve them in business processes.
Business Ethical Values: It is important to act in accordance with ethical values and to adhere to the principles of honesty, transparency and reliability. We are
committed to acting honestly and fairly towards our customers and all our stakeholders.
Risk Management: We identify and analyze the risks in our business processes and implement efficient risk management policies and procedures. We continuously 
conduct risk assessments to minimize customer and operational risks.

CUSTOMER SATISFACTION POLICY
Customer Orientation: We aim to fully understand and meet customer needs and expectations by adopting a customer-oriented approach in all our pre- and post-
sales processes.
Transparent and Quality Service: We provide our customers with open, transparent, reliable and high quality service in accordance with their needs and
expectations.
Customer Feedback and Complaint Management: We handle all kinds of feedback and complaints from our customers in a transparent, impartial, objective, fair and
confidential manner, and aim to provide effective and permanent solutions.
Accessibility and Communication: We continuously provide an effective communication infrastructure where our customers can easily communicate their feedback.
Measurement and Continuous Improvement: We regularly measure customer satisfaction through surveys, feedback analyses and performance indicators, and
improve our processes by evaluating these data within the scope of continuous improvement.
Complaint Prevention and Permanent Solutions: We produce permanent solutions by performing root cause analyzes to prevent the recurrence of customer
complaints.
Legal and Regulatory Compliance: We provide all our services in compliance with relevant legal legislation and regulatory requirements.
Sustainability and Efficiency: We develop innovative, efficient and quality solutions to provide customer-oriented, sustainable service.
Participatory and Innovative Approach: We create a working environment that supports the participation of our stakeholders, encourages creativity and supports
change.
